# Communications and Launch Plan

## Purpose

Communications and launch plan defines how the benchmark announces launches,
major updates, seasons, reports, and correction-sensitive changes.

## Launch Checklist

- final methodology and benchmark card
- current release notes
- validated public data export
- accessible leaderboard and run pages
- FAQ and submission guide
- incident contact and takedown contact
- rollback plan
- embargo list if any
- social and blog copy
- public caveats reviewed
- source links and citation text

## Announcement Types

Use separate templates for initial launch, season launch, result announcement,
technical report, white paper, incident correction, deprecation, and roadmap
update.

## Rollback

Launch-blocking issues can pause publication, hide provisional pages, retract
embeds, or replace homepage modules with maintenance state. Rollback preserves
audit history.

## Coordination

Embargoed parties receive the same public-safe materials and release time.
No party receives hidden-test details or unpublished scoring advantages.

## Research Basis

Google SRE launch checklist practice motivates pre-launch readiness, rollout
planning, and rollback preparation. Keep a Changelog motivates linking public
communication to concrete release records.

# Onboarding for Evaluators

## Purpose

Onboarding for evaluators prepares reviewers to score official runs
consistently, safely, and with the right evidence standards.

## Training Modules

- benchmark purpose and scope
- milestone ladder and rubric
- evidence model
- public versus hidden checks
- calibration-suite practice
- privacy and redaction boundaries
- conflict disclosure
- evaluator notes
- disagreement and adjudication
- appeals and corrections

## Signoff Requirements

Evaluators complete practice scoring on calibration runs, review common scoring
mistakes, acknowledge conflict policy, and receive maintainer signoff before
scoring official submissions.

## Review Quality

Evaluator notes must cite evidence, explain uncertainty, avoid private hidden
details in public summaries, and distinguish code quality from milestone proof.

## Refresher Training

Rubric changes, new milestones, scorer drift, or incident findings can trigger
refresher training.

## Research Basis

ACM artifact review practice motivates structured artifact evaluation and clear
review criteria. Inter-rater reliability practice motivates calibration,
double-scoring, and adjudication.

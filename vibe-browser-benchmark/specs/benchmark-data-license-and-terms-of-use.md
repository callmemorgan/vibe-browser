# Benchmark Data License and Terms of Use

## Purpose

Benchmark data license and terms of use explain how public datasets, result
cards, reports, charts, badges, and artifact metadata can be reused.

## Covered Materials

- public data exports
- result cards
- benchmark cards
- report tables and charts
- public artifact metadata
- public screenshots where licensed
- badges and embeds
- methodology text where separately licensed

## Required Terms

Terms specify license, attribution, redistribution, commercial use,
modification, warranty disclaimer, prohibited misrepresentation, trademark
limits, artifact-download risks, and takedown contact.

## Attribution

Reuse should cite benchmark name, season or export ID, source URL, license,
access date where useful, and whether data was modified.

## Misrepresentation

Users must not remove official/provisional/non-comparable labels, imply
endorsement, or present modified exports as official benchmark data.

## Research Basis

Creative Commons guidance motivates clear attribution and modification notices.
DataCite and FAIR practices motivate machine-readable license metadata for
public datasets.
